New Signing: Star Midfielder Wesley Ogam Joins AGAPE FC

We are thrilled to announce the signing of international midfielder Wesley Ogam from European champions!

AGAPE FC is delighted to confirm the arrival of Wesley Ogam on a four-year contract. The 26-year-old attacking midfielder brings a wealth of experience, having represented his national team on 45 occasions and scored crucial goals in major tournaments.

Ogam was instrumental in his previous club's success, registering impressive statistics with 18 goals and 22 assists in all competitions last season. His creative flair, vision, and technical ability will add significant firepower to our midfield.

"I'm incredibly excited to join AGAPE FC. The club's ambition matches my own, and I can't wait to contribute to our success on the pitch. The fans' passion is legendary, and I look forward to creating special moments together."

Wesley Ogam

Head Coach Lazarus Kingoo commented: "Wesley is a world-class talent who will elevate our team to new heights. His experience at the highest level and winning mentality align perfectly with our goals this season."

Wesley will wear the iconic number 10 shirt and is expected to make his debut in our upcoming fixture against Warriors FC on March 5th.

Epic 4-0 Victory Against Matuu Heroes FC - Match Report

AGAPE FC delivered an outstanding performance with a commanding 4-0 win over Matuu Heroes FC at our home stadium.

It was a night to remember at the AGAPE Stadium as our team put on a masterclass performance, dominating from start to finish. Muema Striker was the star of the show, completing his hat-trick with clinical finishing that left the opposition defense helpless.

Match Summary:

  • 12' - GOAL! Muema Striker opens the scoring with a powerful header
  • 34' - GOAL! Striker doubles the lead with a brilliant solo effort
  • 56' - GOAL! Hat-trick hero Striker completes his treble from the penalty spot
  • 78' - GOAL! Kamumo Flash adds the fourth with a stunning long-range strike

The victory takes us to second place in the league table, just two points behind the leaders with games in hand. The atmosphere was electric, with over 40,000 fans creating an unforgettable atmosphere.
