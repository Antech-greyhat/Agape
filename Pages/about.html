About AGAPE FC

Our Story, Our Values, Our Journey

Our History

AGAPE FC was founded in 2020 with a vision to create a football club that represents excellence, unity, and community spirit.

From humble beginnings in local leagues, we have grown into a formidable force in professional football. Our journey has been marked by dedication, perseverance, and an unwavering commitment to the beautiful game.

Over the years, we've built a legacy of success, nurturing talented players, and creating unforgettable moments for our passionate fans. Today, AGAPE FC stands as a symbol of sporting excellence and community pride.

Our Mission

To inspire and unite our community through exceptional football, fostering talent, promoting sportsmanship, and creating lasting memories for players and fans alike.

Our Vision

To become a leading football club recognized for sporting excellence, youth development, and positive community impact, setting new standards both on and off the pitch.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Passion

We love the game and play with heart, determination, and enthusiasm in every match.

Unity

Together we are stronger. We build team spirit and support each other on and off the field.

Excellence

We strive for greatness in everything we do, continuously improving and setting high standards.

Respect

We honor the game, our opponents, officials, and fans with respect and fair play.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas, tactics, and technologies to stay ahead in modern football.

Community

We are proud to serve and give back to our community, making a positive difference.
